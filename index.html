The Solo Show

Rogue

"You're in safe hands… with this Rogue."

A fast, off-beat and heartfelt ride through wholesome chaos, unplanned adventures, and embracing a life that's 'off-script' — on purpose. Georgie politely and accidentally rebels against the norm: she'll fly interstate just to get a haircut, her best friends are in their 70s, and the words 'full-time job' make her shiver.

Bold, quirky and completely authentic — a story you'll laugh at, cheer for, and leave thinking "of course she did that." You never know what to expect. Neither does she. That's the fun of it.

Meet Georgina

Off-script,
on purpose

Georgie dives headfirst into life's absurdities — finding comedy, chaos and heart in the most unexpected people and places. She's the kind of girl who goes out for lunch and ends up in an inflatable lobster costume selling fish for a random seafood restaurant. (Genuinely. Last Tuesday.)

"The Thriller from Millaa Millaa" is a North-Queensland-raised comedian known for her fast-paced, light-hearted and quirky humour. A two-time RAW Comedy State Finalist, Georgina has toured regional Australia, performed solo shows in major cities and — her personal favourite — hosted her town's inaugural Celebrity Milking Competition. She's also been heard on ABC Radio and 4BC Radio, and is a regular at the Sit Down Comedy Club.
