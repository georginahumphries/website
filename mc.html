Good to know

FAQ

What kinds of events do you host?
Conferences, summits, awards nights, galas, end-of-year and Christmas parties, product launches, expos, fundraisers, festivals and weddings — anywhere a confident, funny host keeps things moving.
Can you keep it clean / corporate-friendly?
Absolutely. Clean-on-request is the default for corporate work — still genuinely funny, just safe for any audience and brand.
Do you tailor the material to our company?
Always. A briefing call covers your team, brand, agenda and any in-jokes, so the hosting feels made for your room — never recycled.
How does booking work?
Send an enquiry with your date, location and event type. You'll get availability and a quote, then a briefing call to lock in the details.
Where do you travel?
Based in Brisbane and available Australia-wide. Virtual and hybrid events are welcome too.
